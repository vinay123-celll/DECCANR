Dr.Ahammad Basha : CEO (Founder)

Our Founder and CEO, Dr.Ahammad Basha have extensive 15+ years of experience in Advanced powertrain and controls including Electric vehicle and Hybrid Electric vehicles. He is an Alumni of IIT Madras and hold PhD degree from Michigan Tech University, USA in the field of ‘Design and Development of advanced powertrains and controls’. He has 3+ years of working experience in Hero Moto Corp Pvt Ltd (Formerly known as Hero Honda) R&D and worked on various powertrain developmental work. He has SAE Bendix award from Society of Automotive Engineering for the best ‘Embedded controller design’ Worked for General Motors in the USA, on Volt Gen-II powertrain and autonomous controller development. Co-founder of Ntropy Enterprises Pvt Ltd, start-up. During the R&D development work at Hero MotoCorp, obtained the certifications on Engine design with 2D and 3D simulations and validations at AVL, Graz, Austria. Worked for 9+ years on EV and HEV vehicle developments in USA
